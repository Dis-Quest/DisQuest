DisQuest privacy

The following data is processed:

  • Cookies: a Cloudflare cookie will be used to make sure you aren't a robot, and a session cookie will be used to keep you signed in.
  • Any data you submit, such as setting an invite link, will cause your Discord user ID to be tied to the invite link.
  • You may request your data at any time by emailing us at the contact page.

We do not track you, nor do we sell your data.
